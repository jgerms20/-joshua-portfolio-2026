Case Study

BMW:
Zeus & Hera

The Ultimate Electric Driving Machine. Mythology meets the future of mobility.

Client BMW North America
Agency Goodby Silverstein & Partners
Role Communications Strategist
Year 2022
Scope Super Bowl Campaign

The Challenge

BMW was entering the electric vehicle market with the iX. But the EV space was crowded with tech-first messaging from Tesla and new entrants. BMW needed to reassert its brand DNA—"The Ultimate Driving Machine"—in an electric context.

The question: How do you prove that BMW hasn't lost its soul in the transition to electric?

The Insight

For centuries, lightning has been synonymous with power, awe, and the divine. Zeus wielded it. Now BMW harnesses it. The parallel was irresistible: if Zeus had to give up his thunderbolt, where would all that power go?

Into the BMW iX.

The Execution

We recruited Arnold Schwarzenegger and Salma Hayek to play Zeus and Hera—Greek gods forced into retirement in a world that's gone electric. The spot premiered during Super Bowl LVI and featured Zeus discovering his new source of power: the all-electric BMW iX.

The campaign extended across digital, social, and experiential, with Zeus "taking over" BMW's social channels and appearing at key automotive events.

My Role

As Communications Strategist at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners, I contributed to the cultural strategy that amplified "Zeus & Hera" beyond the Super Bowl broadcast. I helped map the pre-launch buzz strategy—identifying teaser moments and earned media opportunities that would build anticipation before the big game.

I developed social listening frameworks that allowed our team to respond in real-time to viewer reactions, turning organic conversation into amplification opportunities. I also contributed to the post-game strategy, ensuring we extended the campaign's momentum across digital platforms in the days following the Super Bowl. The spot became a viral sensation with over 50 million views in its first week—proving BMW's electric future is just as thrilling as its legendary past.

The Impact

Zeus & Hera became one of the most memorable Super Bowl campaigns of 2022. More importantly, it successfully repositioned BMW's electric offerings as worthy successors to the brand's legendary performance heritage—proof that going electric doesn't mean going boring.
