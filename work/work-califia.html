Plant-Based Movement

Califia Farms:
Calilujah!

A joyful celebration of plant-based living. Every sip is a hallelujah moment.

Client Califia Farms
Agency Goodby, Silverstein & Partners
Role Communications Strategist
Year 2023
Scope National Campaign

The Challenge

Plant-based alternatives have become mainstream, but the category is crowded with functional messaging about health benefits and sustainability. Califia Farms needed to break through with something more emotional.

How do you make plant-based feel less like a compromise and more like a celebration?

The Insight

Switching to plant-based isn't a sacrifice—it's a discovery. That first sip of creamy oat milk in your morning coffee. The rich foam that actually holds. The taste that makes you wonder why you ever needed dairy.

These are hallelujah moments. Moments of pure, unexpected joy.

The Work

"Calilujah!" transformed the brand's messaging from functional to emotional. We created a vibrant visual and verbal identity that celebrated the pure joy of plant-based living.

"Calilujah! It's that moment when plant-based doesn't just work—it transcends."
— Campaign Manifesto

My Role

As Communications Strategist at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners, I developed the audience strategy that brought "Calilujah!" to the right people at the right moments. I dug into research to understand the eco-conscious consumer mindset—what motivates someone to reach for oat milk instead of dairy, where they spend their media time, and what messages resonate beyond functional benefits.

I identified the key platforms and content environments where our joyful, celebratory tone would land best—curating a mix of streaming, social, and digital placements that matched the campaign's energy. I also contributed to influencer recommendations, focusing on lifestyle creators who could authentically champion the plant-based movement without feeling preachy. The work helped position Califia Farms not just as an alternative, but as a first choice for modern consumers seeking both taste and values alignment.

The Impact

The campaign elevated Califia Farms' brand perception from functional alternative to joyful lifestyle choice. It connected with consumers on an emotional level, driving both awareness and consideration in the competitive plant-based market.
