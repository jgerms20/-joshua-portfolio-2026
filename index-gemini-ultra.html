About Me

Born and raised in South Carolina, I've always been a curious cat. Whether it's a book in my hand, a podcast in my ears, or a camera at my side, I've always taken joy in learning more about the world around me and what makes it go round.

This curiosity has guided me through every stage of my life; from being an active member of the newspaper club in high school, to studying Journalism & Mass Communications at the University of South Carolina, where I minored in Information Science and served as assistant sports editor for The Daily Gamecock. It eventually led me into the world of advertising, where I've built a career in Strategy at some of the top agencies in the United States.

Now, living in Los Angeles, California, my curiosity fuels each of my creative pursuits: photography, podcasting, creative writing, movies & TV and building with Artificial Intelligence. I'm drawn to authentic storytelling that captures the present moment while honoring the past and building the future.

MY JOURNEY

Origin

Columbia, SC

University of South Carolina

Born and raised in the capital of South Carolina. Studied Journalism & Mass Communications with a minor in Information Science. Served as assistant sports editor for The Daily Gamecock. This is where my curiosity for storytelling first took root.

2021–23

Charlotte, NC

Home Base

My physical home during the remote era. Charlotte served as my base while I worked with agencies across the country. Built a home studio, explored the city's growing food scene, and stayed close to family in South Carolina.

2021–23

San Francisco

Goodby Silverstein & Partners

Worked remotely with one of the most legendary agencies in advertising. Traveled to SF for extended periods, contributing to campaigns for BMW, Pepsi, and more. Learned the craft of cultural strategy from the best in the business—all while maintaining Charlotte as my home base.

2023–24

Portland, OR

Wieden+Kennedy

Joined the legendary Wieden+Kennedy as Media & Comms Supervisor. Worked on the Cannes Grand Prix-winning DoorDash "All The Ads" Super Bowl campaign. Experienced the Pacific Northwest's creative energy and coffee culture.

2024–Present

Los Angeles, CA

TBWA\Chiat\Day

Currently serving as Senior Connection Strategist at the Disruption Company. Leading work on Levi's x Beyoncé, Sephora, and DIRECTV. Living the California dream while building the next chapter of my career in the entertainment capital of the world.
