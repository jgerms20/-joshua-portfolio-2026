Super Bowl LVII

Samuel Adams:
Brighter Boston

Kevin Garnett brings Boston pride to the Super Bowl. A celebration of the city that brews it all.

Client Boston Beer Company
Agency Goodby, Silverstein & Partners
Role Communications Strategist
Year 2023
Scope Super Bowl Campaign

The Challenge

Samuel Adams is Boston's beer. But in a Super Bowl crowded with celebrity cameos and high-budget spectacles, how do you cut through the noise while staying true to your roots?

The brand needed to celebrate Boston's unique spirit—the pride, the grit, the unwavering loyalty—in a way that felt authentic to Sam Adams drinkers everywhere.

The Insight

Boston isn't just a city—it's a state of mind. And nobody embodies that more than the legends who've worn its colors. Kevin Garnett brought a championship to Boston and became part of its fabric forever.

As a lifelong Celtics fan, I pitched the idea of partnering with KG to bring "Your Cousin" to life. His intensity, his passion, his unmistakable Boston energy—it was the perfect match.

The Work

Directed by Aaron Stoller, "Your Cousin's Brighter Boston" launched during Super Bowl LVII. The spot aired in 15 markets and was supported by a robust media plan across TV, digital, and OOH.

My Role

As Communications Strategist at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners, I helped bring this campaign to life—and I'm especially proud that I pitched partnering with NBA legend Kevin Garnett. As a lifelong Celtics fan who grew up watching KG's legendary intensity, I knew his authentic Boston pride would perfectly embody the city's spirit. When the idea landed, it was one of those moments that reminded me why I got into this industry.

I contributed to the regional media strategy that maximized our Super Bowl LVII investment, developing the approach that targeted 15 key markets where Boston pride runs deep. I helped identify the OOH placements that would turn the campaign into a local celebration—from billboards along the Pike to bar takeovers in Faneuil Hall. The work extended beyond game day into an ongoing conversation with Boston's passionate community, proving that authentic local storytelling can punch above its weight even on advertising's biggest stage.

"Boston doesn't just make beer. Boston makes Samuel Adams. And that's a whole different thing."
— Campaign Manifesto

The Impact

The campaign captured the essence of Boston pride and resonated with viewers across the country. Kevin Garnett's authentic connection to the city brought credibility and emotion that no script could manufacture.

The spot drove significant conversation on social media and reinforced Samuel Adams' position as the beer that embodies Boston's fighting spirit.
